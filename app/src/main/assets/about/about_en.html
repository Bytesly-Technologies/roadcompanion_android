Manage civic parking services and save on extra parking fees!

Road Companion notifies its subscribers via an audible signal about the termination of zonal parking period and reminds to turn off the parking timer in order to avoid extra payment. Notification about the parking period termination is automatically sent to the customer as soon as the vehicle leaves the parking zone.

Users can select from the settings page how many voice reminders to receive after each completed parking period in order to promptly deactivate the parking timer.

Road Companion app also enables its customers to save regularly used parking lots.
